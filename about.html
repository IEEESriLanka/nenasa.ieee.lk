About Us


Nenasa (නැණස) is a volunteer-driven initiative by the IEEE Sri Lanka Section SIGHT, dedicated to transforming STEM education across Sri Lanka. We believe every student, regardless of background, deserves the opportunity to explore science, technology, engineering, and mathematics in an engaging, practical, and inspiring way.

Through workshops, training programs, mobile labs, and community partnerships, Nenasa empowers both students and teachers with the tools, knowledge, and confidence to thrive in the digital age.

Together, we’re building a future where STEM is for everyone.
