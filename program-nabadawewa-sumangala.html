Robotics and Arduino Practical Session




On June 14th, 2024, the third workshop of the Nenasa STEM Education Workshop Series was held at A/Sri Sumangala Maha Vidyalaya, Nabadawewa. This event aimed to foster a deeper understanding of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) among students, equipping them with essential skills for the future.

The event began with a welcome from the Vice Principal and a speech by the Principal, who emphasized the importance of the workshop for student development. Chamodi, Chairperson of Nenasa, inspired students to pursue their passions and explore opportunities in STEM. The workshop featured a robotics introduction and Arduino practical session led by Madhushan, followed by an interactive session by Nimeshika.

The positive feedback from participants reflected the event's profound impact, reinforcing our commitment to fostering a culture of continuous learning and innovation, not only enriching students' knowledge but also strengthening community ties, paving the way for brighter futures in STEM and beyond.
