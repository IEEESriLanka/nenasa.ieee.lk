Sparking Creativity and Innovation


On June 14th, 2024, at 12:30 PM, an inspiring program was held at Arawwala Dharmapala Vidyalaya with the participation of Nenansa members – Chamodi, Hashini, and Abhishek. The event aimed to motivate students, introduce new ideas, and encourage them to actively take part in innovation and creativity. A total of 53 students (28 girls and 25 boys) along with 3 teachers joined the session, making it a lively and engaging afternoon.

The program began with an introductory speech by a student, who confidently opened the event and outlined the schedule for the day. This was followed by a welcome speech from the Vice Principal, who highlighted the importance of student participation and how engaging in activities like these can contribute to personal growth and future opportunities.

Chamodi, the Chairperson of Nenansa, delivered an inspiring address. She encouraged students to chase their passions and reminded them about the many opportunities waiting for those who are willing to learn and explore different fields. Her words set a motivating tone and made the audience feel excited for what was coming next.

With the formalities complete, the workshop commenced under the guidance of Ms. Praveena. One of the key highlights of her session was initiating an Inventors Club at the school. This idea created a lot of excitement among the students, as it provided them with a platform to collaborate, innovate, and put their creative ideas into action beyond the classroom.

Finally, the program came to a close with a heartfelt Vote of Thanks by a student, appreciating the efforts of everyone who contributed to making the event successful. The day was not only educational but also uplifting, leaving both students and teachers inspired to continue learning and innovating.
