Introduction to Robotics and Arduino Workshop


On August 16th, 2024, a special program was held at Badulla Central College to introduce students to robotics, Arduino and innovation. The event was organized with the support of Nenasa participants including Madhushan, Disara, Chamodi and Abhishek. The program began with an introductory speech by Mr. Vinidu. He welcomed the students warmly and explained what they could expect throughout the day.

Chamodi, the Chairperson of Nenasa, also spoke to the students. She encouraged them to follow their passions and to believe in their potential. Her message reminded everyone about the importance of learning and trying new things.

The main sessions were conducted by Madhushan who explained the basics of Robotics and Arduino in a clear and engaging way. Students took part in a hands-on activity where they built robots and worked together as a team. The organizers donated Arduino kits to the school, giving students a chance to keep learning even after the event. The program ended with a Vote of Thanks from one of the teachers who appreciated the effort of everyone involved. It was an inspiring and educational experience that motivated students to explore the world of technology.
